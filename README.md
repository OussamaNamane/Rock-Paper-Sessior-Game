This is a console-based Rock Paper Scissors game written in C++.

It allows the player to play against the computer for multiple rounds, and shows the winner of each round as well as the final winner of the game. The game also changes console colors based on who wins each round for a more interactive experience.

---

## Features

- Play 1 to 10 rounds per game.
- Player vs Computer mode.
- Displays round results and the final winner.
- Console color highlights for the winner:
  - Green: Player wins
  - Red: Computer wins
  - Yellow: Draw
- Option to play again after the game ends.

---

## How to Play

1. Run the program.
2. Enter the number of rounds you want to play (1-10).
3. For each round, choose one of the following:
   - Stone [1]
   - Paper [2]
   - Scissors [3]
4. The computer will randomly make its choice.
5. The winner of the round will be displayed.
6. After all rounds, the final winner will be announced.
7. You can choose to play again.
